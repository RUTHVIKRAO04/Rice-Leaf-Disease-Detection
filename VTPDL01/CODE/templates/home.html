About This System

Timely and accurate detection of leaf diseases in rice plants is crucial for sustaining crop productivity and ensuring food security. Traditional methods for identifying plant diseases are often time-consuming, labor-intensive, and require expert knowledge, which limits their scalability in large-scale agricultural environments.

This advanced system leverages deep learning (DenseNet121) to detect rice leaf diseases from high-resolution leaf images with exceptional accuracy. Our AI model has been trained on thousands of rice leaf images and can identify 6 different disease categories.
